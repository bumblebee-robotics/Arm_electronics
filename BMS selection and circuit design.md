## BMS Selection
![[Drawing 2026-02-28 01.32.58.excalidraw]]
*using 18650 cell of 18mm diameter*
**Geometry of a single row**
$A_{s}=\frac{\pi}{4}(18^2) = 81\pi$
2S $\implies$ $2A_{s}=2(81)\pi=162\pi$
**Capacity**
Capacity of a single cell $=1200$ mAh
$\implies$ Energy per 2S $2(1200)=2400$ mAh
*assume using a 2S1P battery pack*
